Pizza Bread Recipe

Home Cuisine Pizza Bread Recipe

Share

Pizza-Bread

Ingredients

  1. Chicken

  2. 2 Tsp Oil

  3. 1 1/2 cup Pizza sauce

  4. 1/2 cup Tomato paste

  5. Tandoori masala powder

  6. Garlic paste

  7. 1/4 tsp Pepper, salt , seasoning pizza, paprika

  8. Bread

  9. 1 Onion

  10. 1 cup cheese

  11. 1/2 cup mozzarella

  12. 1 cup Butter

  13. garlic

Directions

  1. Follow instructions as per video or written directions are given just below the video

    2. Chicken marinate

  2. 4 tsp lemon juice

  3. 1 tsp Tandoori masala powder

  4. 1/2 tsp yogurt

  5. 1/2 tsp pepper

  6. 1/4 tsp salt

  7. 1/4 tsp chili powder

  8. Garlic and ginger paste

  9. Mix ingredients well. Refrigerate for 30 mins

    10. Pizza bread Preparation

  10. In a pan on medium heat, add oil and chicken marinations

  11. Let cook for 3 - 4 min

  12. Add pizza sauce, tomato paste, 1tsp tandoori powder, garlic paste. Cook and stir for 1 min.

  13. Add pepper, salt, seasoning pizza, paprika.Cook for 5-7 mins and put aside.

  14. Slice the bread. Add butter and garlic paste, chicken curry, onion, cheese and mozzarella

  15. Bake for 10 mins❤
