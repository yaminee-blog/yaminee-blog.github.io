Vegetarian Briani

Home Cuisine Vegetarian Briani

Ingredients

  1. 4 cups basmati rice
  2. 6 cardamon
  3. 4 cloves
  4. 3 cinnamon stick
  5. 2 1/2 tablespoon briyani spices mix
    (easily available in grocery stores)
    e.g lazzat biryani spices or stardeg biryani spices
  6. 8 onions, finely sliced
  7. 6 potatoes, cut into quarters
  8. 3 large carrots, cut lengthwise
  9. Raw jack fruit
  10. 1 cup soya
  11. 1 cup green peas
  12. 2 cups green beans, cut lengthwise
  13. 3 tablespoon ginger paste
  14. 3 tablespoon garlic paste
  15. 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  16. 1/2 cup finely chopped mint
  17. 1 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
  18. 1/2 teaspoon orange food color
  19. 1 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
  20. Oil for frying
  21. Water
  22. Salt
  23. Jafran

Directions (45 mins)
Share

    For the rice

  1. Wash rice and soak in water for 30 minutes

    2. For the vegetables

  2. Season the carrots, green beans, raw jack fruit, soya with salt separately

  3. For the potatoes while seasoning with salt add a pinch of food color

  4. Heat oil in a frying pan

  5. First fry the onions, as they turn to a light golden color remove and put aside

  6. Then fry the potatoes, cook till they turn to a light golden color, remove and put aside

  7. Similarly fry the soya and as they turn to a light brown color, remove and put aside

  8. Fry the raw jack fruit for 3-5 minutes, remove and put aside

  9. In the same pan fry the carrots for 3-5 minutes, remove and put aside

  10. Fry the green beans for 3-5 minutes, remove and put aside

    11. For the final assembly

  11. In a large utensil, combine all the vegetables i.e potatoes, carrots, green beans, raw jack fruit and soya

  12. Add ginger, garlic paste, yogurt, half of the fried onions, the cloves, cardamon cinnamon sticks, briyani spices and salt

  13. Mix well

  14. Use an oven proof large utensil that has a cover to assemble the briyani

  15. Add 1 teaspoon ghee at the bottom of the utensil

  16. Add the marinated vegetables to the large utensil.

  17. Add rice, onto the marinated vegetables, followed by 5 cups of water. Add 2 teaspoon of salt.

  18. Add some peas, fried onions, food color, chopped coriander and mint leaves on the top.

  19. Soak the jafran for 5 mins and sprinkle over.

  20. With the help of a spoon make some holes in the utensil at different places and pour some orange colour.

  21. Put the lid and cook for 30 minutes

  22. To serve the briyani, remove the layers little by little in a separate bowl

  23. Mix well by taking extra care not to mash vegetables

  24. Serve with a vegetable salad and a chutney or pickles.
