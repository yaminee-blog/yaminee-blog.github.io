Homemade Pizzas

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. 1 tbs baking powder
  3. 4 tbs milk powder
  4. 1 tbs sugar
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 3 tbs oil

Directions

  1. Sieve flour, milk powder and baking powder, then add sugar and salt.

  2. Then add water till it form a dough. Add 2 tbs of oil, keep on mixing, keep some flour aside so that u can add if it sticks too much.

  3. Keep mixing, add the other tbs of oil, will well till it doesnâ€™t stick anymore . . .

  4. Preheat oven 180 degree, grease your pan, oil and lil flour will do. Then place the dough on it so it takes the form of the pan, don't forget to use a fork to make small holes all over the dough. Very important.

  5. You can put whatever you please for the topping. Be creative .

  6. I choose 3 cheese pizza, you can put as much as u want, pizza has no limits but make sure u put mozzarella on top and origano as finishing.

  7. For the tomato sauce, u take half of a small can (rougail regal or pasta sause regal) And add any spices you wish.

  8. Mix it with thick cream, then apply on the dough, then cheddar, brie cheese then mozzarella . . .

  9. Let it cook for 20min to 30min.

    10. And yummy yum. The cheesy pizza is here
