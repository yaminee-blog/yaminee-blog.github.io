Garlic Butter Shrimp

Home Cuisine Garlic Butter Shrimp

Servings: 3

Ingredients

  1. Shrimp
  2. 5 cloves of garlic crushed or sliced
  3. 1/2 Butter
  4. Salt,pepper,paprika to taste
  5. 1/4 cup chicken stock
  6. Cream
  7. Chopped coriander and spring onion

Directions (15 mins)
Share

  1. Follow instructions as per video or written directions are given just below the video

  2. In a pan melt half of the butter

  3. Add the shrimp, paprika, pepper and salt.

  4. Let cook for 5 mins remove and set aside.

  5. Add the rest of the butter ,add garlic saute for a minute and chicken stock bring to a boil until reduce in half.

  6. Add lemon juice, Pepper and cream.

  7. Add the shrimp, chopped coriander and spring onion.
