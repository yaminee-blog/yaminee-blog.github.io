Time to reconnect

Exactly one year ago, the Earth took a break and now after several months of normality on our little island, here we are back again into confinement whereby this time the number of cases is increasing exponentially by day.

However, compared to last time, we now have a knowhow of handling situations. Protocols being established and contact tracing being done more rapidly. Companies have already contacted their employees for WAP or working from home. People have adapted and evolved their lifestyle with COVID-19.

On a personal level, I feel less anxious and more oriented. I believe that now is it time again to reconnect with our friends and families. I remember to have seen loads of artists collaborating and coming out with amazing works. Creativity exploding everywhere. I do hope to see that again.

Until then I hope everyone is safe at home and pray that there is less cases of domestic violence.
